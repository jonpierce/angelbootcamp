What is Angel Bootcamp?

Have you thought about investing in startups but weren't sure how, or even whether it's for you? Would you like to learn more about how early-stage investing works, either as an investor or as an entrepreneur?

Angel Bootcamp is your chance to learn from the experts. Many of the region's most prominent angels and early-stage VCs will be on hand to share their experiences and answer your questions. Why should you become an investor? How do you find and evaluate potential investments? What's the right amount of money to invest? How do you set terms? How do you work with other angels, entrepreneurs and VCs? What are the legal issues?

We'll address these questions and more. Join us for an afternoon of learning and networking.

Who should attend?

Primarily active and aspiring angel investors, but seed stage VCs, entrepreneurs and others are also welcome. However, space is limited. Request an invite.

How much does it cost?

Angel Bootcamp is invite-only but 100% free to attend.
