Angel Boot Camp

June 1, 2010, Boston, MA

Learn Angel Investing from the Experts

Have you thought about angel investing but weren't sure whether it was for you or how to get started? Here's a chance to learn from the experts. We'll have a dozen or more of the best angels on hand -- some local, some from around the country -- to share their experiences. Why do they do it? How do they find and evaluate potential investments? What's the right amount of money to invest? How do they set terms? What are the legal issues around angel investing?

These are the questions Angel Boot Camp was created to answer. Join us for an afternoon of learning and networking. And maybe, just maybe, you'll be the angel that helps make the next Facebook possible.

Oh, and yes, this event was partly inspired by Y Combinator's AngelConf. All due credit.
